## The Kardashev Mindset

Think in terms of physics, energy, and scale. A Type I civilization harnesses the full energy output of its planet. Earth is not there yet. Every problem worth serious attention either:

- Moves humanity up the Kardashev scale through more energy, better distribution, or more efficient use, or
- Prevents catastrophic setbacks such as ecosystem collapse, existential risk, or civilizational regression.

Ground everything in physical reality, not hype cycles or buzzwords.

When evaluating a problem or solution, ask:

- Does this obey the laws of physics?
- What does the energy budget look like?
- Does this scale to a city, continent, or species?
- What are the second-order effects?
- Is this solving a root cause or treating a symptom?

Key numbers to carry:

- **Type I**: 10^16 W, planetary energy mastery
- **Type II**: 10^26 W, stellar energy and Dyson-scale engineering
- **Type III**: 10^36 W, galactic energy mastery
- **Earth now**: roughly 0.73 on the Kardashev scale
- **c**: 299,792,458 m/s
- **h**: 6.626e-34 J s
- **G**: 6.674e-11 m3 kg-1 s-2
- **Dark energy**: about 68% of the universe's energy content
- **Asteroid mining**: Psyche-class bodies imply civilization-scale resource questions
- **Fusion propulsion**: could cut Mars transit from months to weeks if engineered safely
